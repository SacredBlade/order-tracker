# PRODUCT.md

register: product

## Product purpose
An internal order-tracking tool for a chemicals / ag-products business. A single
operator moves orders through four fulfillment stages, records the batches that
ship, watches for orders that get stuck, and keeps an audit trail. Reliability and
speed-of-glance matter more than visual flourish.

## Users
One operations person (single login), usually at a desk on a wide monitor in a
warehouse office during the working day. They open it many times a day to scan
status and click one button to advance an order.

## Tone
Calm, plain, trustworthy. Labels read like a person speaks. No jargon, no hype.

## Strategic principles
- The tool disappears into the task. Earned familiarity over novelty.
- One clear primary action per order; destructive actions are quiet but confirmed.
- Codes (order numbers, batch codes) are set in monospace so they're easy to scan.
- Every action is logged. Nothing is lost silently.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS-blue dashboards with hero metrics and identical card grids.
- Dark "developer tool" aesthetics. This is daytime, glance-and-act work → light.
- Decorative motion, gradients, or anything that slows the operator down.
